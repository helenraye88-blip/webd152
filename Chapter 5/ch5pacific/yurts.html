The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures that offer the comfort of a hotel with the beauty of nature.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt has a queen-size bed, comfortable seating, and a private bath.
What should I bring?
Bring your hiking gear, camera, and sense of adventure!
